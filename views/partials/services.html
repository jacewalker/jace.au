FAQ

Common questions.

What happens if you get sick or go on holiday?

All code is version-controlled, documented, and deployed to your infrastructure. If I disappeared tomorrow, any competent developer could pick up where I left off. That said — I communicate proactively and plan around any time off.

How do AI agents actually work in your process?

Think of me as a director with a film crew. I set the vision and strategy. My AI agents generate design variations, write and review code, run tests, optimise assets, and audit quality. Every output is reviewed and refined by me before it reaches you.

Do I own the code?

Absolutely. You own 100% of the code, design, and assets. Everything is delivered in a git repository that you control. No lock-in, no proprietary platforms, no vendor traps.

How fast can you deliver?

A standard marketing website takes 1–3 weeks. Web applications take 2–6 weeks. iOS apps take 4–10 weeks. My AI agents work around the clock, so you get results faster than traditional agencies.

What's included in the revisions?

Revisions cover design and content changes within the agreed scope. Each round includes a review period where you provide consolidated feedback. Small tweaks outside of formal rounds are always accommodated.
