UniFi Mass Adoption

I'm quite a lazy person and manually adopting Ubiquiti gear by launching the Ubiquiti Chrome Tool and giving each device 2-4mins of attention to adopt was not something I enjoyed too often.

I had to automate it. This tool will discover and adopt all UniFi devices on the network within a few seconds. All concurrently.

This tool is currently private and cannot be viewed.
